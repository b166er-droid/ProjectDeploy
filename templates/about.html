Application Title:

This application was inspired by IMDb as such, the group settled on the acronym ABCD as the title and logo.
ABCD stands for Application Based Cinematography Database.

Implemented Features:

  • Register & Login functionality
  • Can rate movies from a scale of 1 to 5 stars
  • Allows users to leave comments
  • Functional and logical navigation bar
  • Information on all movies provided
  • Clicking on a movies poster, redirects to the movie's website
